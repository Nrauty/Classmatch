HOW TO FIND YOUR MATCH

Let's get started using ClassMatch.

  • Step 1:

    Sign In/Up

    Create an account to connect with other students, find study partners, and explore campus events. Already have an account? Just log in to start matching, messaging, and managing your student life—all in one place.

  • Step 2:

    Create Your Profile

    Tell us about yourself! Add your age, major, hometown, and study preferences. Your profile helps us match you with the right study partners and connect you with others on campus.

  • Step 3:

    Complete Our Questionaire

    Answer a few quick questions to help us understand your study habits, interests, and campus involvement. This helps us recommend better matches and events tailored to you!

  • Step 4:

    Match and MeetUp/Study

    Get matched with students who share your study habits and availability. Once matched, you can chat, plan meetups, and collaborate to stay on track and succeed together.
